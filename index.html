Polyhouse Vegetable Production Workflow Anand Agro Farms Project:BENF[Benevolent Farms]

1. Cultivation in Polyhouse

  • Prepare soil and install irrigation systems
  • Plant selected vegetable varieties
  • Maintain optimal growing conditions (temperature, humidity, light)
  • Apply fertilizers and pest control measures as needed
  • Monitor plant growth and health

2. Harvesting

  • Determine optimal harvest time for each vegetable type
  • Hand-pick vegetables at peak ripeness
  • Collect harvested vegetables in clean, food-grade containers
  • Transport containers to the sorting and grading area

3. Initial Cleaning

  • Remove any dirt, debris, or damaged parts
  • Gently wash vegetables if required, depending on the type
  • Allow vegetables to dry if washed

4. Grading and Sorting

  • Inspect vegetables for quality, size, color, and shape
  • Sort vegetables into different grades (e.g., Premium, Grade A, Grade B)
  • Remove any substandard or damaged produce
  • Record quantities for each grade

5. Packaging

  • Select appropriate packaging materials (e.g., crates, boxes, bags)
  • Package vegetables according to grade and type
  • Ensure proper ventilation in packaging to maintain freshness
  • Label packages with relevant information (grade, weight, date)

6. Quality Control

  • Conduct random checks on packaged vegetables
  • Verify adherence to quality standards and packaging requirements
  • Make adjustments to the process if necessary

7. Storage

  • Move packaged vegetables to a temperature-controlled storage area
  • Organize storage by vegetable type and grade
  • Monitor storage conditions (temperature, humidity) regularly

8. Distribution

  • Prepare orders for various clients (e.g., supermarkets, restaurants, wholesalers)
  • Load vehicles with correct orders, ensuring proper handling
  • Maintain the cold chain during transportation

9. Record Keeping and Analysis

  • Document all processes, quantities, and quality metrics
  • Analyze data to identify trends and areas for improvement
  • Use insights to optimize future production cycles

10. Continuous Improvement

  • Regularly review and update the workflow
  • Invest in new technologies or methods to enhance efficiency
  • Train staff on best practices and any process changes
